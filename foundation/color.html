Color Palette

A rich, earthy palette inspired by nature. These colors provide the visual foundation for the "Sweater Weather" aesthetic.

Earth Tones

Primary brand color. Used for headers, footers, and heavy structural elements.
Persimmon

Primary action color. Used for Buttons, Call-to-Actions, and active links.
Rust

Secondary action color. Used for hover states and strong accents.
Golden Hour

Accent color. Used for icons, illustrations, and highlighting key information.
