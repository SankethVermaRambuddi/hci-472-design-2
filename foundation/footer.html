Footer

The footer shows copyright information and the last time the website was updated.

Example

Copyright © 2025 Sanketh Verma. All Rights Reserved.

Code

<footer> <div class="container"> <div class="row"> <div class="col"> <p class="footer-body">Copyright © 2025 Sanketh Verma. All Rights Reserved.</p> </div> </div> </div> </footer>

Usage

  • The footer must stay at the bottom of every page.
  • The footer should include copyright information and the last year the website was updated.
  • Additional links like Privacy Policy or Terms of Service can be included if necessary.

Accessibility Considerations

The high-contrast color pairing of Charcoal text on a Mist background was chosen to maximize visibility. This ensures that essential copyright and legal information remains distinct and legible for users with low vision or color blindness.
